* Overview

The CAN-CODE-BIZ Ecosystem Coordination Strategy transforms a portfolio of
126+ software projects spanning 147M+ lines of code into an interconnected
intelligence network. This document serves as the central strategic alignment
and synchronization hub for all ecosystem projects, agents, and business
development initiatives.

** Strategic Vision

Transform the project collection from isolated applications into a coordinated
community learning ecosystem where:
- Projects inform each other through shared patterns and learnings
- Resources optimize across boundaries rather than within silos
- Community value and business sustainability compound through strategic relationships
- Intelligence emerges from meta-analysis shared openly with the community
- Open-source contributions serve as primary goal, creating tools for all
- Learning and teaching happen together - collective growth over individual gain
